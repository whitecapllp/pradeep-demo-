SWAN is a BIM consultancy committed to simplifying construction workflows through intelligent automation and collaborative design. We act as the bridge between designers, clients, and architects—ensuring projects run efficiently from concept to completion.

From BIM execution plans to supply chain assessments, audits, and training, we empower teams with the right tools, strategies, and technology to deliver excellence. Our consultants oversee budgets, mentor staff, and drive digital innovation.

Expert in Facade, Architecture, Interior, Structure, and Landscape—powered by parametric modeling, BIM coordination, data insights, AI design, and Dynamo scripting. Based in West Bengal, India, with projects spanning the globe.
